Below different presentations of "2013-11-08T21:12:52.000Z"

Formats datetime value in local format:

Wed Nov 9 2013 00:12:52 GMT+0300

You can omit time completely:

Wed Nov 9 2013 00:12:52 GMT+0300

Show the timezone:

Wed Nov 9 2013 00:12:52 GMT+0300

Force 24-hour time for any locale:

Wed Nov 9 2013 00:12:52 GMT+0300
